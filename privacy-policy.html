Introduction

At TopFortuneKings.com, we take the protection of your personal data seriously. This GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data, as well as your rights as a data subject. We respect the rights of individuals to control their personal data, including the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of their personal data. We also provide transparent and clear information about our data processing activities through this privacy policy. This statement outlines the company's commitment to complying with GDPR and ensuring the protection of personal data, as well as providing a clear and transparent explanation of their data processing activities.

Data Collection

We collect personal data from you when you create an account, place an order, or contact us for support. This data may include your name, address, email address, phone number, payment information, and order history.

Data Use

We use your personal data to process and deliver your orders, to communicate with you about your orders and our products, and to provide you with customer support. We may also use your data to personalize your experience on our website, to send marketing communications if you have given us consent to do so, and to improve our website and services.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Data Protection

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. We use secure servers and encryption to protect your data during transmission, and we restrict access to your data to authorized personnel only.

Data Subject Rights

You have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to its processing, and the right to data portability. You also have the right to withdraw your consent to our processing of your data at any time.

Data Controller and Contact Information

TopFortuneKings.com is the data controller of your personal data. If you have any questions or concerns regarding our GDPR or the processing of your personal data, please contact us at info@topfortunekings.com. We will respond to your request as soon as possible, but no later than one month from the date of receipt.
