Delivery info

We offer free shipping for all orders within Italy. Orders will be processed within 1-2 business days and shipped via courier service. Delivery times may vary depending on your location, but generally, you can expect to receive your order within 3-5 business days.

For international orders, shipping fees will apply and will be calculated at checkout. Please note that international orders may be subject to customs and import duties, which will be the responsibility of the customer.

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information so you can monitor your shipment's progress.

Please ensure that the shipping address you provide is accurate and complete to ensure a smooth delivery. If your package is returned to us due to an incorrect or incomplete address, additional shipping fees may apply.

If you have any questions about shipping, please contact our customer service team at support@topfortunekings.com.
