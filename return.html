Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your diamond jewelry purchase from topfortunekings.com. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return your item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, exchange, or store credit.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused and in the same condition that you received it, with all original packaging and tags intact. Please note that personalized or custom-made items are not eligible for return.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team by email at returns@topfortunekings.com or by phone at +39 390 654 0113. We will provide you with instructions on how to return your item, including the shipping address and any applicable fees.

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item to ensure that it meets our return policy requirements. If approved, we will process your refund, exchange, or store credit within 10 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method, minus any shipping fees or restocking fees, if applicable.

Please note that we are not responsible for any loss or damage to the item during shipping. We recommend using a trackable shipping method and purchasing shipping insurance for the full value of the item.

If you have any questions or concerns about our return policy, please don't hesitate to contact us. We are here to help you find the perfect diamond jewelry for your needs and provide a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience.
